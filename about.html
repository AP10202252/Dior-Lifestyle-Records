About DIOR LIFESTYLE RECORDS

We cultivate icons—not trends. Discover the ethos, mission, and vision driving our movement.

Dior Lifestyle Records was founded by Khakhu Junior Nemamilwe, Luis Abernethy, and Tristan Jacobsen with one goal: craft an environment where raw talent meets world-class resources, turning underground visionaries into chart‑topping trailblazers.

At the heart of our academy is a belief that the youth represent the future of music. Talent alone isn’t enough. That’s why we prioritise structure, discipline, and authenticity—because too many people sit on their gifts. Here, we live up to our potential and always aim higher.

While many artists come through our academy, we also sign raw talent on sight. We’re still underrated—but not for long. Dior Lifestyle is building legacies through collaboration, partnerships, and a relentless pursuit of greatness.

Our Vision

We believe in real stories, real music, real growth. Our vision: push our artists into the spotlight and build a community that thrives on collaboration and innovation. Whether you’re an artist, producer, or creative visionary—Dior Lifestyle Records is where ambition becomes legacy.
