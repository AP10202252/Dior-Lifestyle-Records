Our Mission

DIOR Lifestyle Records exists to nurture, develop, and elevate the next generation of iconic artists and visionary hit-makers. We are more than just a record label — we are a movement, a creative sanctuary, and a launchpad for raw talent ready to break through the noise of the industry.

We empower artists with the tools, knowledge, and environment needed to fully realize their potential. Whether you're a rapper finding your voice, a singer refining your sound, or a producer crafting the next genre-defining beat, we help you rise above the ordinary and define your legacy.

We provide access to cutting-edge facilities — from fully equipped recording studios and mixing rooms to writing spaces designed for creative breakthroughs. But tools alone don’t make legends — mindset does. That’s why we offer hands-on coaching from experienced mentors, producers, and engineers who understand what it takes to go from underground talent to household name. We don’t just teach technique — we instill discipline, vision, and creative fearlessness.

At the heart of DIOR Lifestyle is a culture of relentless creativity. Our artists are encouraged to experiment, push their limits, and challenge the status quo. We don’t follow trends — we set them. This is a space where bold ideas are welcomed, where storytelling matters, and where every track is treated as a masterpiece in the making.

We build not just careers, but lasting legacies. Every artist who walks through our doors becomes part of a growing family — a community of dreamers and doers committed to supporting one another and leaving a mark on music history. We’re not here for fleeting fame; we’re here for timeless impact.

DIOR Lifestyle Records is where dreams become reality, where talent turns into greatness, and where music is not just made — it’s lived.

🔥 Real Highlights

Founders Luis Abernathy, Mfanelo Rikhotso, Tristan Jacobsen and Kay Jay the Quiet Kid who freestyled the hit single “WAR” in just 17 minutes—moving 7,000 units in its first week without a major label push. That relentless energy fuels everything we do.

From recording freestyles on his phone for hours to building a full studio and launching DIOR Lifestyle Records and its academy, Kay Jay’s grind started young. He began rapping at age 12, and by 19 he was already being recognized for his raw talent, unique flows, and work ethic that sets him apart from the rest.

Kay Jay recorded songs like “Renegade Freestyle” in a single take and “Ball Till I Fall” with a hook that became a fan anthem. Every song blends emotion, aggression, and truth—built without a pen, built from life.

Today, DIOR Lifestyle is more than a label—it’s a movement. Under Kay Jay’s leadership, we’ve launched music that resonates across South Africa and beyond, while giving independent artists the tools and mentorship to break through on their own terms.

This is not industry-standard. This is legend-building.

Success Stories

From viral breakout tracks to sold‑out showcases, DIOR Lifestyle Records has become a launchpad for unstoppable talent. What started as an independent dream has grown into a movement shaping the future of South African music—one artist at a time.
